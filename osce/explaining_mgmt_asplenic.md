# EXPLAINING MANAGEMENT: ASPLENIC PATIENT

### BEFORE 

Vaccinations:

- Hep B/A

- Pneumovax - every 5 years 

- Meningococcal B + C (one off)

- Influenza (yearly)

### AFTER

Prophylactic Pen V

- If allergic give macrolide i.e. erythromycin

Safety netting 

- If you get sore throat/stiff neck --> go to GP 

Complications:

- Pain 

- Infection 

- Lung collapse 

- Gastric ileus
