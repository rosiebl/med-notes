# GRAM STAINING

#### Gram positive cocci:

- Staphylococcus spp (clusters)
- Streptococcus spp (chains)
- Enterococcus spp


#### Gram positive bacilli:

- Clostridium spp
- Corynebacterium spp
- Listeria spp
- Actinomyces

#### Gram negative cocci:

- Neisseria spp
- Moraxella catarrhalis
